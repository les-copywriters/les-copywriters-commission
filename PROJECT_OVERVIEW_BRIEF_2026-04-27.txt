LES COPYWRITERS COMMISSION
Brief Project Overview
Date: 2026-04-27

This project is a business dashboard used to manage sales commissions, team performance, and sales operations in one place.

At its core, it helps the company:
- track sales and commissions
- monitor refunds and unpaid deals
- manage closers and setters
- view performance analytics
- sync data automatically from external platforms
- review call performance and coaching data
- use AI tools to support sales coaching and analysis

The main parts of the project are:

1. Commission management
- stores sales
- calculates closer and setter commissions
- tracks payment type and transaction details

2. Team management
- shows performance for closers and setters
- allows admins to manage team members and roles
- gives detail pages for each person

3. Analytics and dashboards
- gives visibility into sales, commissions, trends, and team results
- includes dedicated views for both closers and setters

4. Platform sync
- pulls information from tools like JotForm, Aircall, iClosed, and Fathom
- supports automatic scheduled syncing
- includes health checks to monitor whether the data is coming in correctly

5. Coaching and call analysis
- imports sales calls
- analyzes them with AI
- helps generate coaching insights, frameworks, and closer profiles

6. Admin monitoring
- lets admins check data quality
- highlights discrepancies and sync issues
- provides operational visibility into how healthy the system is

In simple terms, this project is not just a commission tracker.
It is a sales operations and performance platform that combines finance tracking, team management, analytics, call coaching, and automated data syncing into one system.
